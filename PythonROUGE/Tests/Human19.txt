In New York City, an expectant Orthodox Jewish couple heading to the hospital was killed after their livery cab was struck by a driver in a BMW who fled the scene early Sunday. Nathan and Raizy Glauber, both 21 years old, were pronounced dead at hospitals but their baby boy survived after it was delivered by cesarean section. The baby is currently hospitalized and is in serious condition. Police are continuing their search for the driver of the BMW.
