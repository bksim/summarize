154 days after acquiring LucasArts, Disney has dissolved the video game development company. Disney will continue to license the LucasArts assets but will no longer develop the games internally. LucasArts was part of a $4 billion acquisition of Lucasfilm. It developed games based on popular Lucasfilm movies (Star Wars) and other titles: Maniac Mansion, Day Of The Tentacle, Grim Fandango, Full Throttle, The Digg and many more. 150 people have been laid off.
