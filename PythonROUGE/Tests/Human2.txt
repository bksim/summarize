In Ohio, Amanda Berry, Gina DeJesus and Michelle Knight, missing after they were kidnapped 10 years ago when they were in their teens and early 20s, were rescued from their captivity in a Cleveland house on Monday. All 3 women have now been released from a hospital and are reuniting with their families. Police say 3 brothers, ages 50, 52 and 54, are the main suspects in the case. They are expected to be charged soon.
